🐱 Our Residents

Meet the Legendary Cats

⚔️

From fearsome warriors to stealthy shadows — all 42 of our cats are rescues with a unique backstory and personality. All want your love (and maybe your snacks).

Katsuro
⚔️ Samurai Lord

The mightiest warrior of Neko Dojo. Katsuro defeated 99 mice before retiring to guard the café. His honor is unbreakable — unless you bring tuna.

Rank: Shogun
Kage
🥷 Shadow Ninja

You never see Kage coming. One moment the seat is empty — the next, a purring shadow is on your lap. Expert in the art of surprise cuddles and croissant theft.

Rank: Jōnin
Yuki
🌸 Gentle Soul

Yuki sits next to anyone who seems sad and gently headbutts their worries away. Favorite hobby: napping in sunbeams and stealing warmth from your coffee mug.

Rank: Beloved
Tōru
📜 Wise Scholar

Tōru has studied ancient cat wisdom for 500 years. He'll knock your book off the table and sit on it while maintaining eye contact. He contains multitudes.

Rank: Onmyōji
